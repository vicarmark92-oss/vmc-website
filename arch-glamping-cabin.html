Why the Arch Cabin Is the Best Glamping Investment

The arch glamping cabin is one of the most photographed, most booked, and most profitable short-term rental structures available today. A hand-built arch cabin achieves the same visual impact as a $25,000–$80,000 prefab dome for $5,000–$15,000 — often less in Southeast Asia.

Airbnb ROI — Southeast Asia Example

  • Average nightly rate: $80/night
  • Estimated occupancy: 70% (21 nights/month)

  • Estimated payback: ~8–9 months

What Is an Aeroponic Tower Garden?

Aeroponic towers grow plants with their roots suspended in air, misted with a nutrient-rich water solution. No soil. No digging. No weeding. One aeroponic tower can hold 18–28 plants in as little as 2 square metres, producing $60–$150+ worth of produce every week at peak production.

Income Potential

  • 1 Tower: $112–$224 net per month
  • 4 Towers: $448–$896 net per month
  • 10 Towers: $1,120–$2,240 net per month
